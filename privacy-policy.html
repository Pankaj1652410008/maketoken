Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 5, 2025

At EVM Token Creator, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our services to create custom tokens on Ethereum, BNB Chain, Polygon, and other EVM-compatible networks.

1. Information We Collect

  • Wallet address (for interaction with the blockchain)
  • Email address (if you contact us or subscribe)
  • Usage data (e.g. device type, IP address, browser type)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • Provide secure and personalized token creation services
  • Improve user experience and platform performance
  • Respond to customer inquiries and support requests
  • Send updates, if subscribed

3. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information. We do not store your private keys or sensitive wallet credentials.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party tools (like analytics or payment gateways) that comply with GDPR and other privacy standards. Your data is never sold or shared without consent.

5. Cookies

We use cookies to analyze site traffic and personalize content. You can control cookie settings via your browser preferences.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us at privacy@lashusdtgen.com for any requests.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@flashusdtgen.com
